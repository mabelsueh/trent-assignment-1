About Us

Crafting Quality Bespoke Leather Goods


Blank Slate was founded by a group of three friends who met through the common hobby of leathercraft. We specialize in the development, design and crafting of your unique creation, utilizing industry standard tools and the finest leathers from Europe and Japan.

Over here at Blank Slate, we believe in the quality of our workmanship, which is why we offer a lifetime guarantee on all workman defects.
